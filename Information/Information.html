Project

Creating Spaces is an innovative web-based platform designed to assist urban planners, architects, and enthusiasts in generating random designs effortlessly. Creating Spaces provides users with an intuitive tool to explore different design possibilities, fostering creativity and inspiring new ideas in projects. Inspired by the different variantions from fractals in nature we have decide to create a platform exploring the different designs, which are created by using subdivision of polygons. The main goal of our team is to create a web application that generates useful structures as a base for durther designs. Central to our approach is the idea of user empowerment. We envision Creating Spaces as more than just a tool—it’s a springboard for imagination. Users are invited to study the intricate forms generated by our platform, gaining insight into the underlying geometry and envisioning potential enhancements. From there, the possibilities are endless. Users can leverage these designs as a foundation for their own projects, experimenting with different approaches and refining their vision with ease in order to get their final desired results but also start their journey in designing with ease. With Creating Spaces, users can easily transition from exploration to implementation, seamlessly integrating our platform into their design workflow.
